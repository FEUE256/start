Förenta Evolutionernas Universitets Elektronik

FÈUE is an independent research initiative focused on long-term technological development in electronics, operating systems, and theoretical physics. The organization prioritizes rigor, reproducibility, and system-level thinking.

Research Domains

Operating Systems

Low-level system design, service orchestration, and experimental boot architectures with emphasis on determinism and auditability.

Theoretical Physics

Conceptual models and mathematical frameworks, including white hole dynamics and non-standard cosmological assumptions.

Electronics

Hardware–software interaction, firmware boundaries, and minimal interfaces between computation and physical systems.

Active Projects

Fix System Services (FSS)

A modular service framework designed to operate close to the OS layer, emphasizing extensibility and controlled execution.

Project page

WHEP

The White Hole Existence Principle explores hypothetical mechanisms for white holes and their observable implications.

Cyber::Boot
