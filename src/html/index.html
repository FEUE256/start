FÈUE

Förenta Evolutionernas Universitets Elektronik

FÈUE is an independent research initiative focused on electronics, operating systems, and theoretical physics. The work emphasizes system-level thinking, technical precision, and long-term sustainability.

Research Areas

Operating Systems

Low-level systems, boot environments, and modular services with an emphasis on determinism and transparency.

Theoretical Physics

Hypothetical models and mathematical frameworks, including WHEP and white hole dynamics.

Electronics

The boundary between hardware and software, firmware design, and controlled execution environments.

Active Projects

Fix System Services (FSS)

A modular framework for OS-adjacent services.

WHEP

Theoretical research on white holes and cosmology.

Cyber::Boot

An experimental boot and system initialization environment.
